Primary research questions for this study:

1. How does the choice of scenarios (probabilistic vs 'structured' streamflow ensembles) influence the robustness of resulting pareto-approximate solutions? 

2. Can we improve the performance NYC reservoir outcomes (diversion shortages resulting from low-storage, flood frequency downstream, minimum streamflows at downstream Montague flow target location, etc) by re-optimizing the parameters defining the NYC reservoir operations outlined in the FFMP? 

3. How does the inclusion of more storage zones improve the objective performance and/or robustness of the pareto-approximate FFMP operations?
